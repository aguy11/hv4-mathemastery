mathemastery.

The next step in elementary elementary math education.

Join the Revolution

In the classroom.

Using mathemastery, teachers can assign curated curriculums of practice problems on various topics in math ranging from 1st to 5th grade level. These problems are automatically generated by the application, leaving the task of teaching to just a few clicks.

At home.

Mathemastery then allows the student to review content learned in school in their household, providing them with an almost endless source of personally tailored problem on every topic. This ensures the student eventually masters what they have learned in the classroom, ultimately enhancing the educational process and bettering results for the teacher.

On their own.

Mathermastery makes it easy to like loving math - the gamified and personally tailored approach makes sure the student never feels behind, and can progress at their own page while still having fun. Students can always access all the material on mathemastery, no matter if the teacher assigns it or not, in the end making them enjoy math even further and making reaching mastery easy.
